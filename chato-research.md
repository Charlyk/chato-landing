## Pain Point #6: Missed Appointments (No-Shows)

**Intensity: ðŸ”¥ (Medium - secondary for Chato)**

### Evidence

**From Romanian ADOM study (500 dental clinic owners/managers):**
> "Aproximativ 30% din totalul problemelor cu care se confrunta proprietarii si managerii de clinici si cabinete dentare din Romania fac referire la programarile neonorate."

**Causes identified:**
1. Patients don't feel urgency (no pain = easy to postpone)
2. Patients don't understand how much a missed appointment costs the clinic
3. Clinics don't have clear policies for no-shows
4. Patients simply forget
5. Patients don't understand the value of dental treatment

### Why This Matters for Chato

This is a secondary pain point for Chato. While Chato doesn't directly solve no-shows (that requires SMS reminders and policies), it can help by:
- Ensuring initial inquiries are captured (more patients in pipeline)
- Qualifying leads before they book (better quality appointments)
- Educating patients about procedures (increasing perceived value)

### Ad Angle

Not recommended as primary angle for Chato ads. SMS reminder systems solve this more directly.

### Current Solutions Romanian Clinics Use

| Solution | What It Does | Gap |
|----------|--------------|-----|
| DentManager | Practice management, SMS reminders | No AI chat |
| Dentuno | Scheduling, patient records, GDPR | No AI chat |
| Website contact forms | Collect inquiries | Not instant, no conversation |
| Phone | Direct communication | Limited hours, staff capacity |
| Facebook Messenger | Some clinics use it | Manual responses, not 24/7 |

### What Romanian Clinics DON'T Have

- AI-powered chat that understands Romanian naturally
- 24/7 automated responses to common questions
- Intelligent lead qualification before booking
- Chat that knows clinic-specific information (prices, services, schedule)

### Competitive Landscape

| Competitor Type | Example | Weakness |

| Generic chatbots | Template-based bots on websites | Don't understand natural language, button-based only |
